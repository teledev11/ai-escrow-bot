Secure Escrow Service

Our bot acts as a trusted middleman between buyers and sellers, ensuring that:

  • Funds are secured before transactions begin
  • Sellers only get paid after delivering the goods/services
  • Buyers are protected from fraud and scams
  • Disputes can be resolved fairly by the system

Multiple Payment Methods

We support a wide range of payment options:

  • Fiat Payments: Bank transfers, PayPal, Wise
  • Cryptocurrencies: Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT
  • Save your preferred payment methods for quicker transactions
  • Secure payment confirmations and verifications

Transaction Management

Easily manage all your transactions with our bot:

  • Create new transactions with detailed descriptions
  • View your active, completed, and disputed transactions
  • Get notifications for transaction updates
  • Confirm receipt of goods/services with a simple command

Dispute Resolution

Fair and transparent dispute resolution system:

  • Open disputes if goods/services aren't as described
  • Submit evidence to support your case
  • Get fair resolutions from our mediation system
  • Automatic refunds when appropriate

How It Works

For Sellers:
  1. Create a new transaction with the /new command
  2. Specify details, amount, and preferred payment method
  3. Share the transaction ID with your buyer
  4. Deliver the goods/services once payment is confirmed
  5. Receive funds once the buyer confirms receipt
For Buyers:
  1. Find a transaction using the provided ID
  2. Review the transaction details carefully
  3. Fund the escrow using the specified payment method
  4. Confirm your payment with the /confirm_payment command
  5. Receive your goods/services from the seller
  6. Complete the transaction with the /complete command
